EXPERIENCE GOD THROUGH MUSIC
WITH CHORALE MINISTRIES

At Chorale Ministries, we believe music is a divine connection that inspires, uplifts, and brings people closer to God. Join us in worship through melodious symphonies and heartfelt harmonies.

UNITING HEARTS IN PRAISE

Our choir unites voices from diverse backgrounds, all with a shared mission—to glorify God through music. We create an atmosphere of worship that transcends the stage and resonates within every soul.

JOIN OUR JOURNEY OF PRAISE

Whether you are a performer or a listener, Chorale Ministries invites you to be a part of this incredible journey. Feel the presence of God through every note and rhythm.

Our Dedication To Music

The Symphonials is continually motivated to hold events that re-emphasize our dedication to feeding the soul with good music. We are hopeful that the people we minister to, will forever benefit from the soothing nature of sound music, rendered by our dedicated choir. Keep listening.

Annual Concert

Our Annual Concert is a grand celebration that brings together the best of our chorale's performances, showcasing a diverse range of musical genres. Join us as we take you on a musical journey through a blend of classic and contemporary pieces.

Community Outreach

We believe in the power of music to connect and inspire. Our Community Outreach program brings the joy of music to schools, hospitals, and local communities, spreading harmony and uplifting spirits through our performances.

Youth Choir Program

Our Youth Choir Program nurtures the next generation of musical talent, providing young singers with the opportunity to develop their vocal skills, build confidence, and experience the joy of performing as part of a choir.

Music Therapy Sessions

Music has the power to heal and comfort. Our Music Therapy Sessions offer a space for relaxation and emotional expression, helping participants to cope with stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges through the therapeutic power of music.

Christmas Carols

Celebrate the festive season with us as we perform timeless Christmas Carols. Our choir spreads holiday cheer with a blend of traditional and modern carols, creating a warm and joyful atmosphere for everyone.
